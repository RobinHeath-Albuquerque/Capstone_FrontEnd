Albuquerque Valley Harvest

Farm For Life

As the owner of Farm For Life, Ivy Green has been bringing in fresh fruits and vegetables to Albuquerue for over 15 years. A family owned farm located in Lemitar, NM, all of the vegetables she grows are started from seed on our farm either in the field or in one of our green houses. We believe in organic and sustainable growing practices.

Bobby and Beas

Bobby and Bea Jones are new to Albuquerque, having recently moved here from Wisconsin. They love the long growing season here and are looking forward to meeting you!In addition to fruits and vegetables, they are also organic local beekeepers, on a mission to help save the bees and make the world a sweeter place. Their honey is raw and with zero additives, it literally comes straight out of our hives, all located in Albuquerque, and into your jar. Enjoy!

Sweet Caroline

For Caroline Davis, being able to sell her produce at the Farmer's Market is a dream come true. "Ever since I can remember, my parents took me to the farmer's market." She is proud to be able to serve her Albuquerque Community with natural foods.
