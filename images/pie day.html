2021 Event Calendar

Our farmer’s market has special events that are the talk of the town! Join us for farm-fresh dinners, plant sales, unique vendors, and much more.

 

2021 Opening Day

April 3rd 2021

The market is open for another great year of farm fresh food, handmade goods and crafts, and much more! Join us on the lawn of the historic Chatham Mills for a farmer’s market morning on Saturday, April 3rd, 2021.

CALL FOR PARTICIPATION:

If you are interested in speaking, demonstrating a cooking or gardening technique, performing, or want to otherwise share your talents and expertise with the folks who visit the Chatham Mills Farmer’s Market, please let us know. Email the market manager at cmfmmanager@gmail.com and we’ll talk!

 

NON-PROFIT BAKE SALES:

The Chatham Mills Farmer’s Market is also happy to host non-profit groups who want to get their word out and raise some funds through a bake sale. Please contact the market manager at cmfmmanager@gmail.com if you’re interested.

Chatham Mills Farmers’ Market is a growers-only farmer’s market located at Historic Chatham Mills in downtown Pittsboro, NC, featuring a variety of fresh local products.
